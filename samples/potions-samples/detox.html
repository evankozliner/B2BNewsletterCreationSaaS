‚õèÔ∏è Where the real value is in the AI gold rush

"Sell pickaxes during a gold rush."

We're living in the AI gold rush, but most will "go broke" chasing tools and hype.

These are my 3 fav takeaways from Jesse Cunningham's guide to surviving the AI gold rush:

  • Master a core skill (not just tools). With no-code and AI, learning how to build is easy. Knowing what to build and for whom is the actual skill.
  • Your data + audience are your differentiation. True leverage comes from owning your distribution (email list, social following, community, etc.) and data (industry insights, experiences, etc.).
  • Play the long game. AI helps you move fast, but don't forget your business fundamentals... or you'll vibe code your way to nowhere.

Think about it: Yes... AI is commoditizing knowledge, but it doesn't replace trust, relationships, and experience-based insights. So why should people listen to you?
