💻 SEO

Google Completes Rollout of August 2024 Core Update

The August 2024 core update, which began on August 15 and completed on September 3, introduced significant changes to Google Search results, particularly focusing on promoting useful content from small and independent publishers. Following feedback from the previous September 2023 update, which negatively affected many sites, this core update aimed to enhance the quality of search results by prioritizing original and helpful content. Initial fluctuations in rankings, especially in the first few days due to a ranking bug, were noted, and most sites experienced limited recovery. Google emphasized that creators should focus on producing satisfying content for users rather than manipulating search rankings, as adjustments will be influenced by ongoing improvements to website content.
