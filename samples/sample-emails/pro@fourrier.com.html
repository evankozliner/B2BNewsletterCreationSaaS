💻 Tech Consulting

IBM QRadar Customers Transition to Palo Alto Networks Solutions

Palo Alto Networks has completed the acquisition of QRadar for $500 million, facilitating the migration of IBM's Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) platform users to Palo Alto's Cortex XSIAM platform. This transition aims to provide users with a more integrated and automated security operations framework, consolidating various functionalities such as data centralization and threat intelligence. Although Palo Alto Networks will offer ongoing support for on-premises QRadar users, the migration process typically takes around three months for those opting for the SaaS version, with qualified customers receiving complimentary support. This acquisition illustrates the ongoing consolidation trend in the security market, as companies seek to develop larger, more comprehensive solutions for security operations centers.
