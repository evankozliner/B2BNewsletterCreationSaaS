Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much does it cost? +
A: We have a starter plan for $120 a month and growth plan that starts at $249 a month, billed annually. Find our pricing here.
Q: Do you integrate with my email platform? +
A: Yes! We work with all of the popular ones: Mailchimp, Substack, ConvertKit, Beehive, Ghost, LinkedIn Newsletter, Gmail, and more. If we don't officially support your platform, no worries! We'll send a draft to you that you can copy and paste.
Q: How do you write my newsletter? +
A: We work with you to understand your voice, audience, and goals. Then our team of writers creates personalized content that sounds like you. You get to review and approve everything before it goes out.
Q: What if I don't like what you write? +
A: No problem! We want you to love your newsletter. You can request unlimited revisions, and we'll keep working until it's perfect. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
Q: Can I cancel anytime? +
A: Absolutely! There's no long-term contract. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your billing portal.
Q: How quickly can you get started? +
A: We can typically have your first newsletter draft ready within 3-5 business days of onboarding. The setup process is quick and painless!
Q: What's your refund policy? +
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll give you a full refund.
